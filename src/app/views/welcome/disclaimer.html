Welcome to TabSINT
TabSINT is an open source platform for administering tablet based hearing-related tests, as well as general-purpose questionnaires. It is meant to be flexible, easy-to-use, and useful for administrators who manage studies of all sizes.

TabSINT is developed by Creare and available open source on Gitlab.
